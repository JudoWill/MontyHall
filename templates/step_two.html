This is a simple page to play the Monty Hall Game.

The rules are very simple:

  • There are three doors. One has a prize and the other two are losers.
  • The player picks one of three doors.
  • Monty will open one of the other doors.
  • The player then has the oppurtnity to switch thier choice or stick.
  • The player wins if they have chosen the door hiding the prize.
